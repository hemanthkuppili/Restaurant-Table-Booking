Reservation Help

Quick guide to booking a table at Plantopia

1. How to Reserve a Table

  • Select a branch (Dreamwood or Heavengarden) and a date.
  • View the table layout and click on an available table.
  • Fill in your details (name, number of guests, time).
  • Agree to the Terms & Conditions and click Reserve.

2. Reservation Guidelines

  • Tables are held for 15 minutes past your booking time.
  • You can reserve for up to 12 guests. For larger groups, please contact us directly.
  • Some slots may require a refundable deposit (especially peak hours).

3. How to Cancel a Reservation

  • Login using your Google account.
  • Go to Your Orders from the profile dropdown.
  • Click Cancel next to the reservation you want to remove.
  • Note: Cancellations must be made at least 2 hours in advance.
